We have all been there, wanting to apply for a job but haven’t got a good CV or haven't got one at all. Then we search for some tips and advice but they all seem overwhelming. This page will cover some tips to help you in your CV writing process as well as show you some helpful videos for interview prep.


  • Key areas to cover in your CV :


  • Your name, mobile number and e-mail


  • profile, ( keep this part short, as your cover letter would cover this sections)


  • education, ( Write it in descending order and DO NOT include primary schoo/


  • work experience


  • skills, ( Relate this to the job you’re applying to)


  • References, ( tip* write “ available upon request” so if they need it they’ll ask you for it)


  • Make sure your profile isn’t loooong. (Remember that people won’t read it they'll SCAN IT!)
