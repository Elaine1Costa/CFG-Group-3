Time

Time is money, you probably have heard it! So, you might rush in this modern world to achieve your goals...



Organisation is key in time management! Try the Apps or the old diary, if you prefer, and make your routine.


    The good news are that like many other skills, time management can be improved, and here are 10 ways to do this:

  1. Delegate tasks;
  2. Priorize work;
  3. Avoid procrastination;
  4. Schedule tasks;
  5. Avoid stress;
  6. Set up deadlines;
  7. Avoid multitasking;
  8. Start early;
  9. Take some breaks;
  10. Learn to say no: Take a look at your 'To Do' list before taking on extra work.
